Innocence - Happy to be Back from School

.

This is the concluding story of my Google Photography Prize entries in Street Photography. I am really happy that I was able to capture what I wanted to.. The Innocence of subject. Not knowing that they are being photographed, those undisturbed emotions are what that make many photos look as beautiful as they are. Just like little children, whose mind is undisturbed with the daily incidents in the rest of the world. Just looking at those lovely faces gets a smile on our face..which glow with happiness when they get down that school bus, now that school is over! Getting back to home and play is all they (we) want!
