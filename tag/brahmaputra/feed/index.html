Lonely - Feeling alone amongst a billion people

.

After coming back from dreams into the harsh reality I felt very lonely in this world! What if I was alone in tackling all these obstacles! For a few moments a chill of loneliness swept me aside, even though I knew that I was living amongst a billion people here.

Right after capturing the pic “Reality” I saw this elderly person sitting on the roadside along the bank of Brahmaputra. I felt this image would capture and convey my feelings perfectly and here it is! In the background, a very busy road of Guwahati looking deserted at peak time!!

My Second Resolution

I like traveling, so I will make it a point to travel to a new place every weekend – be it somewhere far away or an unexplored neighborhood.As soon as the sun started dipping, I decided to go to the mighty Brahmaputra river that snakes around our Institute and capture the scenic beauty. You see, I like taking pics a lot, photography is my long time hobby.

I shall take you through a photo tour of the place I went, an old temple by the ghats of Brahmaputra. Aboding an ancient statue of Lord Vishnu, Aswaklanta Temple is designated as one of the Archaeological Sites of India. Built in 1720 AD, this temple offers a picturesque view of the river and refreshing peace for our busy urbane minds. Enjoy!
