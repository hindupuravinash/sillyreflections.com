I cannot say it in one sentence other than to say that I love it and I am at peace when I go birding! Perhaps next time, I will come up with some points and describe how it feels when I’m out there so that you can decide if you wanna begin bird watching!

Here is a picture taken right in the middle of a metro city – Chennai! It is a very popular birding place called Pallikkaranai Marsh, often said to be “Green Lungs of the City”, it faces the same threats as other wetlands all over the world – human encroachment, drying up, pollution. But when I stand there watching the birds, I forget the surroundings, the blaring horns, the road-side dust or burning sun, I just keep watching!!
